Membership FAQ

How does booking work?

Standard & VIP: $50 deposit required to book. Must call/text 24 hours before to reschedule. Late or no-show: $50 fee.
Recurring: No deposit but $30 no-show fee applies.

Can I share my membership?

Standard & VIP: Individual use only.
Recurring: For a Fee.

How do I cancel recurring membership?

Must cancel after recurring payment charged and before the 3rd week of payment cycle.

Do unused sessions roll over?

Recurring memberships: Yes, unused sessions roll over to the next month.

What about points?

Standard & VIP: Earn 2.5 points per service. Points carry over when renewing.
Recurring: Earn 5 points per session and unused sessions.

Which membership is best for me?

Standard/VIP: Best if you want no recurring payments and deeper discounts on multiple visits.
Recurring: Best for consistent monthly visits with flexibility.
